Information

Notes before donating:

  • The donor must be in a good health condition in order to donate.
  • Age between 18 to 60 years.
  • Weight must be above 50 kg.
  • Haemoglobin level at least 12 g/dl.
  • Avoid donating if you have been through any proceasure, injection, peircing or tatto recently.
  • You must not be pregnant or breastfeeding.

The above information is stated according to the world health organization.
